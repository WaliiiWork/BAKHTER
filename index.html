Our Services

Bakhter Taxi provides fast, reliable and affordable taxi services in the city. Our drivers are experienced and knowledgeable about the city's streets to ensure you get to your destination on time, every time.

Groups

Perfect for group outings and events, we offer spacious vans and minibuses to accommodate parties of any size. Our professional drivers ensure everyone travels together safely and comfortably, making us ideal for corporate events, weddings, or family gatherings.

Taxi Service

Experience reliable and comfortable daily taxi services for all your transportation needs. Whether it's a short trip across town or a longer journey, our professional drivers provide prompt, safe, and courteous service at competitive rates.

Airport Service

Skip the stress of airport parking and traffic with our dedicated airport transfer service. We offer punctual pickup and drop-off services to all major airports, with flight monitoring and meet-and-greet options available for your convenience.

VIP Transfer

Experience luxury travel at its finest with our VIP transfer service. Featuring premium vehicles and highly trained chauffeurs, we provide an exclusive transportation experience perfect for executives, special occasions, and those who appreciate the finer things in life.

Our Pricing

Metered Pricing

Bakhter Taxi offers metered pricing for those who want to pay for their ride based on the distance traveled. This ensures that you only pay for what you use.

Long Distance

For longer trips, we offer competitive pricing to ensure that you get the best value for your money. Our drivers are experienced in long-distance travel and will get you to your destination safely.

Flat Rates

If you prefer flat rates, Bakhter Taxi offers affordable pricing for those who want to know the cost of their ride upfront. This ensures that there are no surprises when it comes time to pay.
